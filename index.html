  • 💾 Export & Import

Powerful privacy by design, not policy.

🔒
localStorage-only architecture

Habit Ally has no backend, no API, no analytics, no cookies, and no third-party scripts. Your habit data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage - it never leaves your device under any circumstances.

Why we built Habit Ally +

Most habit trackers require an account, sync your data to a server, and monetize through subscriptions or advertising. We wanted something different - a tool that respects your data, works offline, and never asks you to log in.

Habit Ally is the result: a fully private, browser-based habit tracker that stores everything in localStorage. No backend, no cost to run, no incentive to monetize your habits.

✦ The inspiration behind this app

Built on an idea from Ali Abdaal's habit tracking spreadsheet

Habit Ally started as a browser-based reimagining of Ali's Google Sheets habit tracker, the one he shared on YouTube that showed how a simple monthly grid with daily check-ins could be genuinely transformative for building consistency.

His core idea was brilliant: lay out an entire month at a glance, check habits daily, and let the visual pattern do the motivating. We took that same philosophy and built it into a private offline-first web app.

If you haven't discovered Ali's work on productivity his channel and book are genuinely worth your time.
